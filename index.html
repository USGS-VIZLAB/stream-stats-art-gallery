About the STREAM

This is a streamgraph, where one month of time spans from the left of the screen to the right. Each color band is one Hydrologic Unit, or a region of the country which is hydrologically related from the National Hydrography Dataset. left We're using a subset of USGS streamgaging sites in the Hydro Climatic Data Network 2009 for our list of gage sites to call. Each band's width is encoded by the total flow measured by all gages in the HCDN for each of the 31 days displayed on screen.
